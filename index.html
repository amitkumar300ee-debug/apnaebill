1 Data We Collect

ApnaEBill does not collect, transmit, or store your business data on any external server. All information — client names, entries, prices, invoices, business settings, and any uploaded logo — stays only on your device's local storage.

We do not require account sign-up, phone number verification, or email login to use the app.
